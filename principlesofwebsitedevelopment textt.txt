      <h4> Principles Of Website Development Page</h4>
      <p> Website development encompasses a broad range of activities and principles that guide the creationl, design and maintances of different website. Here are the key priciples of website development. 
       <br>
         <ol>
           <li><b>Planning And Strategy:</b> This is the act of understand and listing the purpose of the website, what it is going to be used for and the goal it aim to achieve. Also, Identifying who the target audience are so as to ensure the design meet their needs and preferences.</li>
            <li><b>User Experience:</b> This is to make sure the website is optimized,easy to navigate and understandable by providing intuitive interfaces. Furthermore, ensuring the website is accessible to everyone even to people with disabilities.</li> 
              <li><b> Design Principles:</b> Ensuring content are well organized in a way that draws attention to the most important element first. furthermore, maintain a consistent style and structure across the entire website including colours,layour and fonts.</li>
           <li><b>Technical development:</b> Focuses on what users interact with directly. key technologies include HTML,CSS, and Javascript</li>
           <li><b>Search Engine Optimization(SEO):</b> This are divided into three part.
             <ul><li> First being the On-Page SEO: Making sure optimize individual web pages to rank higher and earn more relevant traffic or search result so as to gain customers and traffic. To acheive this you need to include relevant keywords, tags and having a clean URL structure.</li>
             <li>Second being the Off-page SEO: This involves improving the website domain authority through backlinks and social signals.</li>
            <li> Lastly, being the technical SEO which ensures that the website is techinally sound, including having a sitemaps, strcutued data and no broken links.</li></ul>
             <li><b>Security:</b> This is crucial as senstitive data needs to be secured through encruyption, secure certificates like SSL or TLS and secure user authentication, also providing software update to protect your website against known vulnerabilities. Finally implementing multifactor of authentication to measure and know who to get the control access.</li>
             <li><b>Testing and quality assurance:</b> This includes checking and ensuring the website works correctly on all major browsers. Also conducting usability tests with real uses to identify any problems so as to fix any issues on time.</li>
             <li><b> Legal and ethical considerations:</b> This including focusing and ensuring that the website complies with privacy laws and clearly stating how users data are being handled. furthermore, providing terms and service to inform users of the rules and regulations of them using the site and respecting copyright law and ensuring all content and media used on the website are properly license or owned. </li>
             </ol>
